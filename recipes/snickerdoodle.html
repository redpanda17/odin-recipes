Snickerdoodle

Description

These cookies will satisfy any sweet tooth! Made with supple amounts of cinammon and sugar, this dish is sure to disappear quickly and leave your quests asking for more!

Ingredients

    Topping:
  • 3 Tbsp. white sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. ground cinnamon
    Cookies:
  • 2.5 Cups all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 tsp baking soda
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar, packed
  • 0.5 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup salted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp. pure vanilla extract
    Yield:
  • 3 dozen

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300º F
  2. In a small bowl combine sugar and cinammon for topping. Set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl combine flour, soda and salt. Mix well with a wire whisk and set aside.
  4. In a large bowl blend sugars with an electric mixer set at medium speed. Add the butter, and mix to form a grainy paste. Scrape sides of bowl, then add the eggs and vanilla extract. Mix at medium speed until light and fluffy.
  5. Add the flour mixture and blend at low speed just until combined. Do not overmix. Shape dough into 1-inch balls and roll each ball in cinammon-sugar topping.
  6. Place onto ungreased cookie sheets, 2 inches apart. Bake for 18-20 minutes. Immediately transfer cookies with a spatula to a cool, flat surface.
