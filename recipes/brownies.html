Brownies

Description

The fudgiest homemeade brownies that you will ever experience! Follow this recipe to wow your friends and family with a one-of-a-kind dessert!

Ingredients

  • 1 1⁄2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3⁄4 cup all-purpose flower
  • 2⁄3 cup cocoa powder, sifted if lumpy
  • 1⁄2 cup powdered sugar, sifted if lumpy
  • 1⁄2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3⁄4 teaspoons sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1⁄2 cup canola oil or extra-virgin aolive oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325ºF. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper. Spray the parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, water, and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan (it'll be thick - that's ok) and use a spatula to sooth the top. Bake for 40 to 48 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached. Cool completely before slicing. Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.
